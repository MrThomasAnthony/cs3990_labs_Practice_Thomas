Career Goals

I aspire to work in the tech industry as a software developer, where I can contribute to innovative projects that drive technological advancements and create meaningful solutions to real-world problems. My passion lies in designing and developing software that enhances user experiences, improves efficiency, and fosters positive change in society. I am particularly interested in working with cutting-edge technologies, collaborating with talented teams, and continuously expanding my skill set to stay ahead in this dynamic field. My dream careers include:

  • AI Developer: Working on cutting-edge artificial intelligence solutions.
  • Full-Stack Developer: Building scalable web applications.
  • Game Developer: Creating immersive gaming experiences.

Here are some companies I admire for their innovation, impact, and contributions to the tech industry. These organizations have set benchmarks in software development, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and user-centric design. Their commitment to pushing technological boundaries, fostering creativity, and solving complex challenges inspires me to pursue a career in tech. I aspire to work in an environment that values continuous learning, collaboration, and the pursuit of excellence. Some of the companies I greatly admire include:

  • Google - Known for its innovation and impact on the tech world.
  • Microsoft - A leader in software and technology solutions.
  • Naughty Dog - Renowned for narrative-driven games.

Favorite Books & Movies

Books and movies have always been a source of inspiration for me, shaping my perspective, fueling my creativity, and broadening my understanding of the world. Whether through thought-provoking storytelling, visionary sci-fi concepts, or compelling character journeys, these works have left a lasting impact on me. They have not only entertained me but also influenced my aspirations and the way I approach problem-solving and innovation. Here are some of my favorites that have deeply resonated with me:

Books:

  • The Pragmatic Programmer by Andrew Hunt and David Thomas - A classic for software developers.
  • 1984 by George Orwell - A dystopian masterpiece.
  • Clean Code by Robert C. Martin - Essential for writing better code.

Movies:

  • Inception (2010) - A mind-bending thriller directed by Christopher Nolan.
  • The Social Network (2010) - The story behind Facebook's creation.
  • Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse (2018) - A visually stunning animated movie.
