I’m Jon Jones, a professional MMA fighter with a reputation as one of the greatest to ever compete in the sport. I was born on July 19, 1987, in Rochester, New York, and began my journey in mixed martial arts at a young age. I became the youngest UFC champion in history at 23 years old and have since defended my title multiple times. My skill set is well-rounded, with expertise in striking, wrestling, and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, allowing me to dominate in all aspects of the fight. I’m known for my high fight IQ, athleticism, and ability to adapt and innovate in the Octagon. Throughout my career, I’ve been ranked as one of the top pound-for-pound fighters in the world, and I continue to push myself to be the best.
